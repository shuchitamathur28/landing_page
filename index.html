Course Curriculum

A Month-by-Month Path to Mastery

Six structured months — from contract fundamentals to specialised, high-value agreements.

Build core drafting fundamentals: identifying contract elements, understanding pre-contractual instruments, and hands-on practice reviewing and redrafting a sale deed. Wraps up with introductory negotiation skills.

Move into frequently-used agreement types and international contracting conventions, paired with advanced negotiation techniques used in real client conversations.

Specialise in intellectual property licensing, technology agreements, and website/e-commerce terms — high-demand niches for freelance legal drafters.

Deep dive into property-related contracts including sale deeds, leave & license agreements, gift deeds, and other real estate documentation.

Master business-critical agreements — NDAs, Master Service Agreements, Power of Attorney, Shareholder & Joint Venture agreements — alongside dedicated freelancing training for Upwork, LinkedIn, and Fiverr.
